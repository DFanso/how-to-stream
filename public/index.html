Bun + TypeScript + HLS

Stream small chunks, not giant files.

This page plays an HLS playlist from your local /streams folder. Generate .m3u8 and .ts files with ffmpeg, keep them in the repo, and Bun serves them over plain HTTP.

How it fits together

  • master.m3u8 lists available qualities.
  • Each quality playlist lists timed .ts segments.
  • The player fetches segments over HTTP as playback advances.
